Dr. Sarah Taylor

Neurosurgeon.

MBBS in Neurology, PHD in Neurosurgeon.

Biography

Dr. Sarah Taylor is a highly accomplished neurosurgeon with a distinguished career marked by exceptional expertise and dedication to her field. Holding an MBBS in Neurology and a PhD in Neurosurgery, Dr. Taylor has established herself as a leading figure in the medical community.

With a strong educational foundation and extensive training, Dr. Taylor has honed her skills in diagnosing and treating complex neurological conditions. Her proficiency in both surgical and non-surgical interventions has enabled her to provide comprehensive care to her patients.

Education

PHD degree in Neorology at University of Mediserv (2006)

Master of Neoro Surgery at University of Mediserv (2002)

MBBS degree in Neurosciences at University of Mediserv (2002)

Higher Secondary Certificate at Mediserv collage (1991)

Professional Philosophy

Dr. Taylor is committed to delivering personalized care, staying at the forefront of neurosurgical advancements, and fostering a collaborative environment for her patients and colleagues.

Her approach is built on empathy, expertise, and a passion for improving patient outcomes, making her a trusted figure in her field.
